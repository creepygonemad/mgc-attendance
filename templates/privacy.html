Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Information We Collect

When using the MGC Student Portal, we collect:

  • Student ID and Date of Birth for attendance verification
  • Usage data and analytics information
  • Device and browser information
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide attendance checking services
  • Maintain and improve our website
  • Send important notifications about your attendance
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Ensure the security of our services

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure data storage systems

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your login preferences
  • Analyze site traffic and user behavior
  • Improve our services
  • Provide personalized content

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect, monitor and analyze this information to improve our service's functionality. These third-party service providers have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

6. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to collect data. For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit their privacy policy page.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
