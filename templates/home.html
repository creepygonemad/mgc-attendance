The College

Mar Gregorios College of Arts & Science, Chennai, established in 1997 by the Malankara Syrian Catholic Church, Trivandrum, under the patronage of the Servant of God Archbishop Benedict Mar Gregorios, is a premier institution in Chennai.

The college is affiliated to the University of Madras and is recognized under section 2(f) and 12(B) of the UGC Act. Situated in a sprawling campus of 10 acres at Mogappair, Chennai, the college aims at providing quality education to all sections of society.

UGC Recognized University of Madras Affiliated NAAC Accredited

Vision

To mould intellectually competent, morally upright, socially committed and spiritually inspired young men and women capable of building a better future.

Mission

  • To provide quality higher education to all sections of society
  • To maintain academic excellence through value-based education
  • To promote character formation
  • To develop leadership qualities

Core Values

Academic Excellence

Pursuit of knowledge and skills through rigorous academic standards and innovative teaching-learning methods.

Moral Values

Character formation based on moral and ethical principles, fostering integrity and responsibility.

Social Commitment

Dedication to community service and social responsibility, creating positive change in society.
